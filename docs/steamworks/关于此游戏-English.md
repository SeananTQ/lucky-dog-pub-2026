# About This Game - English

You may have already kept cats, fish, tiny bugs, or all kinds of curious little companions on your desktop.

This time, the one staying by your side is a dog.

[GIF: A small dog peeks out from the corner of the screen and rests its paws near the taskbar, as if it has just been brought to the desktop.]

## Bring a Dog to Your Desktop

Lucky Dog Rise is a warm and relaxing desktop pet game. Your pup does not take over your workspace or interrupt what you are doing. It simply stays in a corner of your screen, keeping you company while you work, study, take a break, or play something else.

As you type, click, and go through your usual computer routine, your dog responds in its own little ways. Those ordinary actions slowly build up Lucky Points, making everyday desktop time feel a little less lonely and a little more lucky.

[GIF: The player types and clicks while the dog reacts from the screen corner, with Lucky Points gently increasing.]

## Take a Break with a Round of Cards

When you want to relax, switch to a light card mode. Choose which cards to keep, draw new ones, and earn more Lucky Points through a mix of judgment, chance, and the quiet bond you build with your pup.

Don't stare only at the cards in your hand. Look at your dog's little expressions from time to time, and you may start to wonder if they are only cute reactions after all.

[GIF: In card mode, the dog sits beside the table while the player keeps or discards cards, and the dog shows different small expressions.]

## Collect Outfits and Build Your Little Nook

Use Lucky Points to open blind boxes and collect new outfits, items, and looks. Make your dog even cuter, customize the player character and background, and turn your desktop into a small lucky nook for you and your pup.

[GIF: A blind box opens to reveal a cosmetic reward, then the scene switches to customization with changes to the dog, player character, and background.]
