# About This Game - English

Maybe you have already kept cats, fish, tiny bugs, or all kinds of curious little companions on your desktop.

This time, it is a little dog that stays by your side.

*Lucky Dog Rise* is a warm and relaxing desktop pet game. You can bring a little dog to your desktop, where it stays in a corner of your screen and keeps you company while you work, take a break, or play games, letting a little luck slip into your everyday routine. Meeting you is the luckiest thing that ever happened to this pup. That is why it is called Lucky Dog.

It will not get in your way or nag you to do anything. It simply stays there, watching you finish one small task after another, cheering you on in its own little ways. When you play other fullscreen games, it can step aside for a while and leave your screen space to the battle ahead.

While you focus on your work, every keystroke and every mouse click gradually builds up Lucky Points. Your everyday actions are no longer just typing and clicking. They become part of your dog's growth and rewards.

When you want to relax, switch to card mode and play a light poker-style mini game with your dog. Choose which cards to keep, draw new ones, and earn more Lucky Points through a little judgment, a little luck, and the quiet bond between you and your pup.

Use Lucky Points to get new blind boxes, collect outfits, items, and fresh looks. Make the desktop dog who has always stayed by your side cuter, more special, and more truly your own companion.

"Busy days come apart at the edges. A little dog stitches them back together."

Leave a spot for this little dog on your busy desktop.
